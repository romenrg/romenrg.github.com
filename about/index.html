About Me

May 3rd, 2018

My name is Romén Rodríguez-Gil and I live in the beautiful island of Tenerife (Spain). Besides being a Software Engineer and Internet Entrepreneur, I am a Lifelong Learner who seeks continuous improvement and cares about the human side of technology.

The digital world has fascinated me since I was very young. Professionally, I have been working in the software industry for more than a decade, wearing many different hats. As a software engineer, I have worked on many different products, teams and technologies. As a manager and entrepreneur, I have led several multidisciplinary teams and worked on business development, innovation management, user experience and digital marketing, learning very valuable lessons. Thanks to these varied experiences I have developed a 360-degree view of the digital industry.

Over the years, I have become a convinced supporter of Agile Software Development, as well as its business counterpart, Lean Startup. Moreover, I am also well aware that “Peopleware” are the key element of knowledge-based companies, although many forget about that. In this sense, as a blogger, speaker and consultant I try to contribute my bit by highlighting the benefits these empirical and more human approaches provide, both for developers and for managers.

Despite all the above, I also love to spend time away from technology, be it having fun with friends, training at the gym or going hiking with my wife.

If you want to know more about my background, you can find me on LinkedIn. You can also follow me on Twitter, where I share more thoughts, news and articles about software, internet and entrepreneurship.
